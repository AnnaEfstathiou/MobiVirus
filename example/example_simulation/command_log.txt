
Command: MobiVirus_Simulator.py -msprime -events 10 -r
-r: Provide the ability to recombinate genomes, if 2 infections happen during a certain time period.
-events: The simulation stops when the given number of events (movements+infections) happpens.
-msprime: Begin the simulation with genomes created by msprime.

The simulation contains 2 strain types: normal and super strains.

Initial Parameters
Number of individuals (n): 20
Initial number of infected individuals (ii): 20
Length of genome (l): 100
Lower bound for the spatial axis (bound_l): 0.0
Upper bound for the spatial axis (bound_h): 6.0
Movement rate (rm): 0.08 (infected), 0.1 (healthy)
Mutation rate for each genome position (r_m): 5e-06
Recombination rate (r_rec): 2e-06
Generations to get a sample (sample_times): 1

Normal strain Parameters
Infection rate (ri_n): 1.0
Infection distance (inf_dist_ns): 0.5
Probability of infection (prob_inf_ns): 0.8
Recovery time (rec_t_ns): 1.0
Immunity time (imm_t_ns): 0.05
Relative infected mobility (rim_ns): 0.5

Super strain Parameters
Number of important genome positions (n_i): 1
Area of the important positions in the viral genome that define the super strain (region_pos): middle
Infection rate (ri_s): 1.5
Infection distance (inf_dist_ss): 0.5
Probability of infection (prob_inf_ss): 0.8
Recovery time (rec_t_ss): 1.0
Immunity time (imm_t_ss): 0.05
Relative infected mobility (rim_ss): 0.5
Event that the super strain mutation is introduced for the 1st time: 0
Period of events when the super strain is introduced (if previously there are no individuals with a super strain): 200

msprime Parameters
msprime parameter: Parameter defining whether to simulate with population demography or not (use_demography): False
msprime parameter: Present-day population size (initial_pop): 10000000
msprime parameter: Past population size (past_pop): 100
msprime parameter: Time (in generations) at which population growth stops and becomes constant (t_gen): 50
